Lake Park

A tranquil lakeside retreat adorned with shimmering reflections under the moonlight, offering a perfect haven for college friends to share laughter and creating memories that dance with the gentle ripples of the water.

Nehru Museum

The Nehru Museum of Science & Technology is situated in the Hijli Saheed Bhavan, formerly the Hijli Detention camp building. Originally built nearly a century ago for the proposed Hijli district headquarters, it was never utilized for that purpose. Today, it serves as a venue for nightouts with friends and society meetings, repurposing its historical significance for contemporary social activities.

Technology Student Gymkhana

Technology Students' Gymkhana is the hub of the numerous extra-curricular and co-curricular activities in IIT Kharagpur ranging from sports to socio-cultural. The Gymkhana is managed by the students, for the students, under the guidance and active participation of the faculty and staff members.
